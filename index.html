Franshesca QM: UX Designer JR.

My passion for visual design comes from my love of reading and writing. To read and write is to imagine and create different worlds, allowing me to explore the dephts of my imagination and bring out new ways to connect and emphatize with people. Through this I am able to find creative and simple solutions for problems in the real world.

After a turn of life changing events at the age of six, my family decided to move to the United States. Seven years later, it was time to go back to my home country, Peru. It was a mixture of excitement and doubt, leaving known things behind and embracing a new journey. Coming back was full of new challenges, such as learning a new language, customs, and dealing with an important family loss. This shaped who I became and made me understand that my goal in life is to find creative and simple solutions for people. That´s my challenge.
